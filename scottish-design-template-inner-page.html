FAQ

Common questions about Denver window tinting

Is window tinting legal in Colorado? +
Yes. Colorado law allows front side windows at 27% VLT or higher, and rear windows at any darkness. We ensure all automotive installs are fully compliant with Colorado state law.
How long does installation take? +
Most residential jobs take 4–8 hours. Commercial projects are scoped per job. Automotive tinting typically takes 2–4 hours depending on the vehicle.
What warranty comes with my installation? +
All our residential and commercial installations include a lifetime manufacturer warranty against peeling, bubbling, and fading. Automotive films carry a limited lifetime warranty.
How much does window tinting cost in Denver? +
Residential film starts around $6–$10 per square foot installed. Automotive starts at $199 for a full vehicle. Commercial pricing is project-based. Request a free quote for an exact estimate.
Do you offer free consultations? +
Yes — all consultations are free. We'll come to your home, office, or dealership, assess your needs, and provide a detailed written estimate at no charge.
How soon can you schedule in Denver? +
We typically have same-week availability in the Denver metro. During peak summer months we recommend booking 1–2 weeks in advance.
